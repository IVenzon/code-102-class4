My Favorite Holiday: Christmas

What even is Christmas?

Christmas day is on December 25th, and it formally celebrates the birth of Jesus Christ. However, Christmas can be enjoyed by anyone and everyone, regardless of what they believe. Whether it's the gifts, the food, or just the holiday spirit, Christmas is a fun time for all. Plus, who doesn't love Santa?

Stuff to do during Christmas

  • Give presents to your friends/family
  • Open presents from your friends/family
  • Sing Christmas carols (poorly)
  • Eat a lot of good food
  • Spend time with your loved ones
